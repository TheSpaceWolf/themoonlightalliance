Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Here are some of the most common questions we receive about The Moonlight Alliance bot.

🛠️ What happened to [X] feature?

With the release of Moonlight 2.0, we retired some older features to streamline performance and focus development on what really matters. If you miss something, let us know! We actively review community feedback, and if enough users support it, there's a good chance it could return.

🚫 Is [someone] banned?

That's private. Moderation decisions are kept strictly between us and the individual involved. You're free to ask them yourself — but if they don't want to talk about it, we won't either.

📨 How can my friend be unbanned?

We don't process ban appeals through third parties. If your friend wants to appeal, they'll need to contact us directly via the HQ server. No proxy requests, no exceptions.

🔓 How can I be unbanned?

Join our HQ server (link available at the top of the page) and open a support ticket. We'll review your case and have a proper conversation. Being polite and honest really helps — rage appeals don't.

🎁 Is there a giveaways command?

Of course! We made a full video tutorial that explains how our amazing giveaways work — check it out on our YouTube Channel (link available at the top of the page). Trust us, it's totally legit. You won't regret clicking it. 😉

✨ GIVE ME MOONLIGHT PLUS!

Absolutely not. Demanding it gets you Plus Blacklisted — no Moonlight Plus for you.

Moonlight Plus is earned, not handed out. Be helpful, active, and actually contribute. If you do, we might notice. But begging? That's a guaranteed no.

🧪 How do I get early access to new features?

Early access (aka Beta Tester Rank) is invite-only. Even staff don't always get it. It's usually granted to long-standing, helpful members of the community who've earned trust. If that sounds like you, feel free to ask — just don't take it personally if the answer is "not yet."

⚠️ Why was I warned, muted, kicked, or banned?

Check your DMs — our system almost always sends a reason. Didn't get one or still confused? Hop into the HQ server and open a ticket. We'll explain or connect you with the moderator who handled the situation.

💻 Who runs Moonlight?

Moonlight is owned by Howlz Digital Studios and developed primarily by The Space Wolf, with web support from MagicGamer. We're not a corporation — just a small, passionate group of creators building cool stuff for our community.

📄 Can I suggest a new feature?

We love suggestions. Use the /suggest command or join our HQ server to drop your idea. Every suggestion is reviewed — just make sure it fits the bot's scope.

🔐 Is my data safe?

Yes — your data is secure. Moonlight doesn't store personal information. We only keep essential data like server IDs, command settings, and config info. Everything is handled securely using standard best practices. For full details, check our Privacy Policy.

Still have questions?

Join our HQ server for direct support from our team! The link to our HQ server is available at the top of the page.
