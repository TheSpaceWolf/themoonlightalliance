The Moonlight Alliance Has Shut Down

Service Permanently Discontinued - Effective Today

Final Service Notice

After careful consideration and due to insurmountable hosting infrastructure challenges, we have made the difficult decision to permanently discontinue The Moonlight Alliance bot service, effective immediately.

What this means:

Bot Services: All TMA bot functionality has been permanently disabled and will not return.
User Data: All user data and server configurations have been securely archived and will be retained according to our privacy policy.
Economy System: All virtual currencies, items, and economy data are no longer accessible.
Server Features: All moderation tools, utilities, and custom configurations are no longer functional.
Website: All website functionality has been disabled except for this final notice page.

Howlz Digital Studios Continues

While The Moonlight Alliance project has come to an end, Howlz Digital Studios remains operational and committed to developing innovative digital solutions.

Our parent company continues to work on new projects and technologies. This closure affects only The Moonlight Alliance bot service and does not impact other Howlz Digital Studios operations or future endeavors.

We encourage our community to stay connected with Howlz Digital Studios for updates on future projects and opportunities.
