The Moonlight Alliance (TMA) is more than just another Discord bot — it's a toolkit born from real needs, crafted by server owners who wanted more from their communities. Whether you're building a close-knit guild or a booming social hub, TMA is designed to give you control, clarity, and convenience.


Is it the perfect fit for every server? Maybe not. But if you're looking for a feature-rich, constantly evolving bot that puts power and customization in your hands, you're in the right place. We built TMA to solve problems we faced ourselves, and now we're sharing it with you — hoping it becomes just as essential to your community as it is to ours.


— TMA Dev, The Space Wolf

Core Features

Moderation

Say goodbye to clunky, limited moderation tools. TMA brings a clean, intuitive moderation system that fits any admin style. Whether you're using Slash Commands, quick-action moderation shortcuts, or automated tools — we've got you covered. Keep your server safe without breaking a sweat.

Utility

Need tools that do more than just the basics? From member management to role automation, server info commands, and productivity utilities — TMA helps you run things smoothly behind the scenes. Give your server structure, without the stress.

Always Growing

TMA is packed with features beyond just moderation and utility — and we're constantly updating it based on community feedback and real-world use. Whether it's fun extras or server-specific tools, there's always something new to discover.
